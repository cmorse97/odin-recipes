Hot Dog Recipe

The 4th of July Classic

There's nothing better than celebrating 4th of July while firing up the grill to endulge in a savory staple like Hot Dogs!

Ingredients

  • Hot Dog buns
  • Hot Dog weiners
  • Mustard
  • Ketchup
  • Relish

Steps

  1. Cook hot dog weiners.

  2. Place hot dog weiner on the hot dog bun.

  3. Dress the hot dog with mustard, ketchup, and relish

  4. ENJOY!
