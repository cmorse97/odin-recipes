Lasagna Recipe

Mom's Classic Lasagna

We are going to dive into how you can lasagna just like mom!

Ingredients
  • 1 lb lean ground beef
  • 12 oz Italian sausage
  • 2 Tbsp extra virgin olive oil
  • Salt and freshly ground black pepper
  • 1/3 cup finely chopped yellow onion
  • 2 cloves garlic, finely minced
  • 2 (29 oz) cans of tomato puree
  • 1 (6 oz) can tomato paste
  • 1 Tbsp granulated sugar
  • 1 Tbsp dried basil
  • 1 tsp dried fennel seeds, crushed
  • 3/4 tsp dried oregano
  • 1/2 tsp dried thyme
  • 1/2 tsp dried marjoram
  • 16 oz lasagna noodles
  • 15 oz part-skim Ricotta cheese
  • 1 large egg
  • 1 1/2 tsp dried parsley
  • 16 oz whole milk mozzarella, shredded
  • 2 oz Parmesan cheese, finely shredded
  • 2 oz Romano cheese, finely shredded
